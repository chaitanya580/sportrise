The Problem

The System
Misses Talent

  • 🔍

    No Verified Coach Directory

    Students rely entirely on word of mouth. Quality coaching is a matter of luck, not merit.

  • 🏆

    Achievements Go Unrecorded

    A student who wins a local tournament has zero digital proof. Scouts never know they exist.

  • 📍

    Tier 2/3 Talent is Invisible

    Scouts physically travel to find players — missing 80% of real talent from smaller cities.

  • 📊

    No Performance Records

    47 crore athletes in India. Only 0.16% carry any digital performance record. Zero data for scouts.
